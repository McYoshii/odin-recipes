Taco Recipe

Description

This taco recipe is a popular Mexican dish that is easy to make and customizable to your taste preferences. The filling can be made with any type of meat, and the toppings can be tailored to your liking.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef or turkey
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 taco shells
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup salsa

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic and saut√© for 2-3 minutes, until softened.
  2. Add the ground beef or turkey to the skillet and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spatula as it cooks.
  3. Add the chili powder, cumin, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet and stir to combine.
  4. Cook the taco shells according to the package instructions.
  5. Fill the taco shells with the meat mixture, and top with shredded lettuce, chopped tomatoes, shredded cheese, sour cream, and salsa.
  6. Serve the tacos hot, and enjoy!
