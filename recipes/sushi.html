Sushi Recipe

Description

This sushi recipe is a Japanese dish that is enjoyed all over the world. Sushi is made by combining vinegared rice with various fillings and toppings, such as fish, vegetables, and eggs. This recipe is for basic sushi rolls, also known as maki.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sushi rice
  • 1 1/4 cups water
  • 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Nori seaweed sheets
  • Assorted fillings (e.g. avocado, cucumber, crab meat, smoked salmon)
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi paste
  • Pickled ginger

Steps

  1. Rinse the sushi rice several times until the water runs clear. Drain the rice in a colander for 30 minutes.
  2. Combine the rice and water in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and simmer, covered, for 15-20 minutes, or until the water is absorbed and the rice is tender.
  3. Remove the rice from the heat and let it sit, covered, for 10 minutes.
  4. Combine the rice vinegar, sugar, and salt in a small saucepan and heat until the sugar dissolves.
  5. Add the vinegar mixture to the rice and stir to combine.
  6. Place a sheet of nori on a sushi mat or a piece of plastic wrap. Spread a thin layer of rice over the nori, leaving a 1-inch border at the top edge.
  7. Arrange the fillings in a line across the center of the rice. Use the sushi mat or plastic wrap to roll the sushi into a tight cylinder, tucking in the fillings as you go.
  8. Use a sharp knife to slice the sushi roll into rounds.
  9. Serve the sushi with soy sauce, wasabi paste, and pickled ginger.
