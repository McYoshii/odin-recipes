Spaghetti Recipe

Description

This spaghetti recipe is a classic Italian dish that is easy to make and delicious. The sauce is made with fresh tomatoes, garlic, and basil, and is topped with Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 large ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook until al dente, about 8-10 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the garlic and saut√© for 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook for 5-7 minutes, until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  4. Stir in the chopped basil and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Drain the cooked spaghetti and add it to the saucepan with the tomato sauce. Toss to coat the spaghetti with the sauce.
  6. Serve the spaghetti hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese.
